Our Ministries

Youth ministry

It's a special branch of ministry that focuses on guiding, teaching, and supporting young people in their faith journey.

Kids Ministry

Children’s ministry in the church is dedicated to nurturing the faith of young children and laying a strong spiritual foundation in their lives. It focuses on teaching the Bible in simple and age-appropriate ways, guiding children to pray, worship, and experience God’s love personally.

Women’s ministry

Our Women’s Ministry is a place where women of all ages come together to grow in faith, build lasting friendships, and serve with purpose. Through prayer, fellowship, Bible study, and community outreach, we encourage and equip women to walk confidently in God’s Word

Bible College

Our Bible College is dedicated to equipping believers with a deep understanding of God’s Word and a strong foundation for ministry
