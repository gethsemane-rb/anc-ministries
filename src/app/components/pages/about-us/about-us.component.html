The Founder - Pastor Issac

Late Pastor Issac, the esteemed founder of ANC Church, was a visionary leader whose life was devoted to spreading faith and building communities. Born and raised in Bangalore, he felt a calling to ministry from an early age and worked tirelessly to share his message of hope and spiritual growth. Through his dedication and unwavering faith, Pastor Issac established 31 churches, each serving as a beacon of light and a center for worship, fellowship, and service. His remarkable leadership, compassion, and commitment to nurturing congregations touched countless lives and left an enduring legacy. Even after his passing, the foundations he laid continue to inspire and guide communities across the region, reflecting his lifelong mission to bring people closer to God.
